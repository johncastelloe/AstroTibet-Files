AstroTibet Help

On the Upcoming tab are listed twenty-one kinds of auspicious days and seventeen inauspicious days for various activities. Among these are favorable and unfavorable days according to the combination of elements: earth, air, fire and water, which impact on the effectiveness of one’s activities as well as one’s health. According to our astrologer Nawang Thartho, “Tibetans rely heavily on the influence of the elements and check them every day.”

The favorable days are listed in order of the most favorable to least favorable; and the unfavorable days in order of least unfavorable to most unfavorable. There are also categories called Unfavorable Day and Very Unfavorable Day, which sometimes fall on one of the favorable element days; their influence is more powerful.

As always, many Tibetan days are either missed or repeated.

According to our astrologers, on Tibetan double days, Tsog should always be done on the first day; Fire pujas should be done only when listed. Naga, Medicine Buddha, Tara, and Protector pujas can be done on both days.

If you must do activities that should be avoided on inauspicious days, Rinpoche advises that practices be done to counteract the obstacles; consult an astrologer or lama about which practices are best.
